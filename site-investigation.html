Comprehensive Land Assessments

Our investigation process follows a tiered approach to identify potential contamination (POTC) based on historical land use and geological sampling.

Phase 1: Desktop Study

Historical search, site walkover, and conceptual site model development to identify risks without breaking ground.

Phase 2: Intrusive Sampling

Physical sampling of soil and groundwater to confirm the presence, extent, and concentration of contaminants.
