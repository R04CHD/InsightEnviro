EPA Compliant Waste Categorisation

In Victoria, all soil destined for off-site disposal must be classified according to its chemical profile. Failure to correctly categorise waste can lead to significant EPA fines, legal liabilities, and project delays.

Our Classification Categories:

  • ✓ Fill Material: Clean soil suitable for unrestricted use.
  • ✓ Category C: Contaminated soil (Low level).
  • ✓ Category B: Contaminated soil (Medium level).
  • ✓ Category A: Highly contaminated (Requires treatment).

The Insight Process

We perform systematic sampling and NATA-accredited laboratory testing (including IWRG 621 parameters) to provide a formal Waste Classification Report ready for the landfill or receiving site.
